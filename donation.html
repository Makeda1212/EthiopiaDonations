Ethiopia is facing a heartbreaking combination of conflict in the north and climate shocks in the south. As violence spreads across northern Ethiopia, the impact it caused is devastating. In the south, prolonged drought has wiped out livestock and has caused displacement. So, it has caused many families to starve and not have anywhere to look for help.

Without access to essential food assistance, malnutrition rates, especially among children, could rise, which will leave them with long-term health consequences. Food aid plays a crucial role in providing immediate relief and addressing food insecurity in vulnerable populations. It helps to bridge the gap between food availability and the needs of those who cannot access or afford adequate nutrition.
